Industrial Shortcut Trap

This upgraded Demo 02 uses real NEU scratches-versus-inclusion images with a prepared shortcut split and learned probes. The baseline is trained on shortcut-correlated images. The intervention keeps the same original images but also sees stamp-randomised and stamp-masked variants of the same parts.

Lesson

The baseline can look excellent on clean cases while still collapsing when the shortcut is swapped or removed. The intervention is only a partial fix, but it does force the model to rely less completely on the shortcut. That change shows up in the challenge-case metrics and in the relative sensitivity of the stamp and part regions.
